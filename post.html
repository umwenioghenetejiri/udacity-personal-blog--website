BOOK REVIEW

Sankofa

Sankofa is an amazing book written by the wonderful Chibundo Onuzo. It is a story about finding one's self, roots and self awareness. I believe everyone shoud read this book.

The story is set in London where the main character is trying to navigate life as a newly separated woman after the death of her mother. Her husband often comes around to visit but she's torn between accepting him back or letting him go.

When she goes through her mother's old files she finds her father's diary. She is shocked to find out about this man just now as old as she is, mother of a twenty -six year old woman.

She begins searching for him and is in for the biggest suprise of her life. Her father is not only alive but is also a man of influence.Will he accept her? Will she get over this imposter-feeling? I guess we will never know until we read Sankofa.
